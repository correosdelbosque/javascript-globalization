## Unit Format

`{ amount: 100, unit: "meter-per-second", form: "short" }` ⟹ "100 m/s"

`{ amount: 59, unit: "second", form: "long" }` ⟹ "59 seconds"

### Duration Format

Unit format for time specifics.

`{ hours: 42, minutes: 23, seconds: 23 }` ⟹ `42:23:23`

`{ hours: 98, minutes: 11 }` in `it` ⟹ `98.11`
